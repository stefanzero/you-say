You Say

by Lauren Daigle

Lauren Ashley Daigle (born September 9, 1991) is an American contemporary Christian music singer and songwriter.

She has released three studio albums:

  • Can It Be (2015),
  • Look Up Child (2018), and
  • Lauren Daigle (2023).

She is known for her soulful voice and has been compared to Adele.
